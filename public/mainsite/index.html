On October 26th, 2013, Hawaii's design community converged on the deck of the Battleship Missouri for a memorable night of friends, food and the festive atmosphere of victory.

The Top 50 winners plus four student winners were announced to a roar of cheers and hand-held noise-makers. We were thrilled to have judges Joe Duffy and Alissa Walker on hand to announce the Top 5, personally explaining what made these pieces the cream of the crop.

A sincere mahalo to everyone who entered. Thanks to you, the competition was fierce and our community can be proud of the design skill and talent that flourishes right here in Hawaii.
